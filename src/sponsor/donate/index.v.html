Online Donation

Tucson Nurses' Week Foundation is a charitable organization that promotes and recognizes outstanding Nursing practices in the greater Tucson area. Everything we do is made possible by your generosity. We host, annually, an educational Conference and Healthy Fair as well as a Gala to celebrate our Tucson Nurses.

Please consider becoming a sponsor of Tucson Nurses' Week! In addition to any donation of your preference, we have many tiers of sponsorship that we encourage you or your organization to consider. All sponsors are recognized on our site, on our social media channels, and at the yearly festivities to show our appreciation of their support.
