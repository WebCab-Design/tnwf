2018 Nurses Week Gala

A formal dance celebrating Nursing, honoring Tucson Fabulous 50 Nurses and presenting the recipient of the annual Clinical Nursing Practice Project Award. Enjoy celebrating, dancing and socializing throughout the evening.

Save the date: Saturday, May 12

No Host Bar Reception: 6 p.m.

Dinner: 7 p.m.

Loews Ventana Canyon Resort - Ballroom 7000 N Resort Dr, Tucson AZ 85750

(520) 299-2020

$75 per person.

Each Fabulous 50 Awardee and his/her guest's admission is complimentary.

Stay at Loews Ventana Canyon Resort for special TNWF pricing

Please Note: All registrations are final. No refunds will be given, however, you may send a substitute attendee for your registration if needed.
