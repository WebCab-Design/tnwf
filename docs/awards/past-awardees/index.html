Fabulous 50 Awardees

To become a member of the Fabulous 50 you have to be an outstanding nurse within and outside of your workplace. These are Nurses who go above and beyond in their professional and personal lives. They, tirelessly, give of themselves to make our community a better place. Tucson Nurses' Week Foundation is proud to present 2016's Fabulous 50 Awardees.
